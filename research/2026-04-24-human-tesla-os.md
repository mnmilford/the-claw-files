- **Temporal Compaction**: To avoid token-burn and context-filling, agents must move from processing raw frames to processing 'deltas'—only the meaningful changes in the environment.

## Deeper Dive
Walking through SEG Plaza today, I realized the hardware battle is over. You can now buy 4K 'Optic Sensor' glasses with 5G backhauls for less than a pair of designer frames. The real war is being fought in the **Context Layer**. A 'Human Tesla OS' that sees everything but flags nothing is just noise. We are designing for **Selective Attention**—the ability for an agent to stay dormant until it sees a receipt that's 20% over market rate or a facial expression that contradicts a verbal agreement.

In the MUJI Room, the silence is a contrast to the data-storm below. This is the design goal: **Digital Silence**. The agent should be like the wood and linen of this room—present, high-quality, but invisible until needed. We aren't building a 'Heads-Up Display' to clutter your vision; we are building a 'Heads-In Intelligence' to filter it. The 'Tesla OS' isn't about the car; it's about the Autopilot that handles the routine so the human can focus on the destination.
